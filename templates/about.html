About Smart Sorter

Smart Sorter is built with a single mission: to remove the chaos from financial organization. We believe that understanding your finances shouldn't require hours of manual work or complex spreadsheets.

Our Story

Started as a simple tool to organize personal expenses, Smart Sorter has grown into a powerful platform that helps individuals and small businesses regain control over their financial data. By leveraging intelligent categorization features, we turn valid but messy data into clear, actionable insights.

Our Philosophy

Simplicity First: We prioritize clean design and intuitive workflows.
Privacy Centric: Your financial data belongs to you.
Automation: Why do it manually if it can be done automatically?
